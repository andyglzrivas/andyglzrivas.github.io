About

I'm Andy Gonzalez Rivas — a sociology student interested in classical theory, political economy, and the social dimensions of technology. This blog is where I publish longer-form thinking that doesn't fit neatly into a seminar paper.

I'm drawn to the tradition of sociological theory that takes ideas seriously as explanatory tools — Durkheim, Weber, and their intellectual descendants. Most of what I write sits at the intersection of that tradition and the very contemporary questions that keep not going away.
